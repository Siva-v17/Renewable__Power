Service FAQs

Common Questions Before You Commit

What happens if my system produces less than the guaranteed yield?

For commercial and utility projects, we guarantee a P90 yield figure in writing. If actual generation falls below that threshold in any 12-month period due to equipment or design issues, we compensate you for the shortfall. The guarantee excludes force majeure events like extended cyclones or grid outages beyond our control.

Can I add battery storage to an existing solar system you didn't install?

Yes, in most cases. We conduct a compatibility audit of your existing inverter and array before proposing a storage retrofit. Most Sungrow and SMA inverters from 2018 onwards support AC-coupled battery integration with minimal modifications.

How does the RESCO model work for commercial clients?

Under a Renewable Energy Service Company model, we own, operate, and maintain the system on your rooftop. You pay a fixed per-unit (₹/kWh) rate — typically 20–35% below your current DISCOM tariff — for 15–25 years. Zero upfront investment from your side, guaranteed savings from day one.

Do you offer a single point of contact for the project?

Yes. Every project is assigned a dedicated Project Manager from survey to commissioning, and a Customer Success Manager for the life of the O&M contract. One person, one number — no bouncing between departments.

What is your average installation timeline?

Residential (3–10 kW): 1–2 install days, 15–30 days for net-meter approval. Commercial (50 kW–1 MW): 2–6 weeks on site, 30–45 days for DISCOM commissioning. Utility scale: 6–18 months depending on land, grid connectivity, and permit complexity.
