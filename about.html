FAQs

Questions We Hear Every Day

How long does a rooftop solar installation take?

For residential systems (3–10 kW), we typically complete the install in 1–2 days once permits are approved. DISCOM net-metering approval adds 15–30 days depending on your distribution company — we manage the entire process on your behalf.

Do you handle TANGEDCO and BESCOM subsidy paperwork?

Yes — completely. Our in-house regulatory team files all PM-Surya Ghar subsidy documents, DISCOM interconnection applications, and net-metering requests. You sign; we do the running around.

What warranty do I get on the system?

Tier-1 modules carry a 25-year linear performance warranty from the manufacturer. Inverters are covered for 10 years. Our workmanship warranty on structure and wiring is 5 years. All warranties are backed by the OEM directly — not just by us.

Can I finance the system instead of paying upfront?

Absolutely. We partner with leading NBFCs to offer solar loans from ₹0 down, typically at 8–10% per annum over 5 years. For most residential customers, the monthly EMI is lower than the electricity bill savings from day one.

Do you work outside Tamil Nadu?

Yes. We currently operate across Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and Kerala. Utility-scale projects span Maharashtra and Rajasthan as well. Contact us with your location and we'll confirm coverage.
